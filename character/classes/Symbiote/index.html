Class Features

As a Symbiote, you gain the following class features.

Hit Points

Hit Dice: 1d8 per Symbiote level
Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Symbiote level after 1st

Proficiencies

Armor: Light armor
Weapons: Simple weapons
Tools: None
Saving Throws: Wisdom, Charisma
Skills: Choose two from Arcana, Deception, History, Intimidation, Investigation, Nature, and Religion

Equipment

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background: (a) a light crossbow and 20 bolts or (b) any simple weapon (a) a component pouch or (b) an arcane focus (a) a scholar's pack or (b) a dungeoneer's pack Leather armor, any simple weapon, and two daggers

Otherworldly Patron

At 1st level, you have struck a bargain with an otherworldly being of your choice: the Archfey, the Fiend, or the Great Old One, each of which is detailed at the end of the class description. Your choice grants you features at 1st level and again at 6th, 10th, and 14th level.

Pact Psionics

Your arcane research and the psionics bestowed on you by your patron have given you facility with psi powers.

Cantrips

You know two cantrips of your choice from the Symbiote psi list. You learn additional Symbiote cantrips of your choice at higher levels, as shown in the Cantrips Known column of the Symbiote table.

psi Slots

The Symbiote table shows how many psi slots you have. The table also shows what the level of those slots is; all of your psi slots are the same level. To cast one of your Symbiote psi powers of 1st level or higher, you must expend a psi slot. You regain all expended psi slots when you finish a short or long rest.
For example, when you are 5th level, you have two 3rd-level psi slots. To cast the 1st-level psi thunderwave, you must spend one of those slots, and you cast it as a 3rd-level psi.

psi powers Known of 1st Level and Higher

At 1st level, you know two 1st-level psi powers of your choice from the Symbiote psi list.
The psi powers Known column of the Symbiote table shows when you learn more Symbiote psi powers of your choice of 1st level and higher. A psi you choose must be of a level no higher than what's shown in the table's Slot Level column for your level. When you reach 6th level, for example, you learn a new Symbiote psi, which can be 1st, 2nd, or 3rd level.
Additionally, when you gain a level in this class, you can choose one of the Symbiote psi powers you know and replace it with another psi from the Symbiote psi list, which also must be of a level for which you have psi slots.

psicasting Ability

Charisma is your psicasting ability for your Symbiote psi powers, so you use your Charisma whenever a psi refers to your psicasting ability. In addition, you use your Charisma modifier when setting the saving throw DC for a Symbiote psi you cast and when making an attack roll with one.

psi save DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier

psi attack modifier = your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier

psicasting Focus

You can use an arcane focus as a psicasting focus for your Symbiote psi powers.

Eldritch Invocations

In your study of occult lore, you have unearthed eldritch invocations, fragments of forbidden knowledge that imbue you with an abiding psionic ability.
At 2nd level, you gain two eldritch invocations of your choice. Your invocation options are detailed at the end of the class description. When you gain certain Symbiote levels, you gain additional invocations of your choice, as shown in the Invocations Known column of the Symbiote table.
Additionally, when you gain a level in this class, you can choose one of the invocations you know and replace it with another invocation that you could learn at that level.

Pact Boon

At 3rd level, your otherworldly patron bestows a gift upon you for your loyal service. You gain one of the following features of your choice.

Pact of the Chain

You learn the find familiar psi and can cast it as a ritual. The psi doesn't count against your number of psi powers known.
When you cast the psi, you can choose one of the normal forms for your familiar or one of the following special forms: imp, pseudodragon, quasit, or sprite.
Additionally, when you take the Attack action, you can forgo one of your own attacks to allow your familiar to make one attack of its own with its reaction.

Pact of the Blade

You can use your action to create a pact weapon in your empty hand. You can choose the form that this melee weapon takes each time you create it. You are proficient with it while you wield it. This weapon counts as psionic for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonpsionic attacks and damage.
Your pact weapon disappears if it is more than 5 feet away from you for 1 minute or more. It also disappears if you use this feature again, if you dismiss the weapon (no action required), or if you die.
You can transform one psionics weapon into your pact weapon by performing a special ritual while you hold the weapon. You perform the ritual over the course of 1 hour, which can be done during a short rest. You can then dismiss the weapon, shunting it into an extradimensional space, and it appears whenever you create your pact weapon thereafter. You can't affect an artifact or a sentient weapon in this way. The weapon ceases being your pact weapon if you die, if you perform the 1-hour ritual on a different weapon, or if you use a 1-hour ritual to break your bond to it. The weapon appears at your feet if it is in the extradimensional space when the bond breaks.

Pact of the Tome

Your patron gives you a grimoire called a Book of Shadows. When you gain this feature, choose three cantrips from any class's psi list (the three needn't be from the same list). While the book is on your person, you can cast those cantrips at will. They don't count against your number of cantrips known. If they don't appear on the Symbiote psi list, they are nonetheless Symbiote psi powers for you.
If you lose your Book of Shadows, you can perform a 1-hour ceremony to receive a replacement from your patron. This ceremony can be performed during a short or long rest, and it destroys the previous book. The book turns to ash when you die.

Ability Score Improvement

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

Mystic Arcanum

At 11th level, your patron bestows upon you a psionic secret called an arcanum. Choose one 6th- level psi from the Symbiote psi list as this arcanum.
You can cast your arcanum psi once without expending a psi slot. You must finish a long rest before you can do so again.
At higher levels, you gain more Symbiote psi powers of your choice that can be cast in this way: one 7th- level psi at 13th level, one 8th-level psi at 15th level, and one 9th-level psi at 17th level. You regain all uses of your Mystic Arcanum when you finish a long rest.

Eldritch Master

At 20th level, you can draw on your inner reserve of mystical power while entreating your patron to regain expended psi slots. You can spend 1 minute entreating your patron for aid to regain all your expended psi slots from your Pact Psionics feature. Once you regain psi slots with this feature, you must finish a long rest before you can do so again.

Eldritch Invocations

If an eldritch invocation has prerequisites, you must meet them to learn it. You can learn the invocation at the same time that you meet its prerequisites. A level prerequisite refers to your level in this class.

Agonizing Blast

Prerequisite: eldritch blast cantrip

When you cast eldritch blast, add your Charisma modifier to the damage it deals on a hit.

Armor of Shadows

You can cast mage armor on yourself at will, without expending a psi slot or material components.

Ascendant Step

Prerequisite: 9th level

You can cast levitate on yourself at will, without expending a psi slot or material components.

Beast Speech

You can cast speak with animals at will, without expending a psi slot.

Beguiling Influence

You gain proficiency in the Deception and Persuasion skills.

Bewitching Whispers

Prerequisite: 7th level

You can cast compulsion once using a Symbiote psi slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.

Book of Ancient Secrets

Prerequisite: Pact of the Tome feature You can now inscribe psionic rituals in your Book of Shadows. Choose two 1st-level psi powers that have the ritual tag from any class's psi list (the two needn't be from the same list). The psi powers appear in the book and don't count against the number of psi powers you know. With your Book of Shadows in hand, you can cast the chosen psi powers as rituals. You can't cast the psi powers except as rituals, unless you've learned them by some other means. You can also cast a Symbiote psi you know as a ritual if it has the ritual tag.
On your adventures, you can add other ritual psi powers to your Book of Shadows. When you find such a psi, you can add it to the book if the psi power's level is equal to or less than half your Symbiote level (rounded up) and if you can spare the time to transcribe the psi. For each level of the psi, the transcription process takes 2 hours and costs 50 gp for the rare inks needed to inscribe it.

Chains of Carceri

Prerequisite: 15th level, Pact of the Chain feature

You can cast hold monster at will—targeting a celestial, fiend, or elemental - without expending a psi slot or material components. You must finish a long rest before you can use this invocation on the same creature again.

Devil's Sight

You can see normally in darkness, both psionic and nonpsionic, to a distance of 120 feet.

Dreadful Word

Prerequisite: 7th level

You can cast confusion once using a Symbiote psi slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.

Eldritch Sight

You can cast detect psionics at will, without expending a psi slot.

Eldritch Spear

Prerequisite: eldritch blast cantrip

When you cast eldritch blast, its range is 300 feet.

Eyes of the Rune Keeper

You can read all writing.

Fiendish Vigor

You can cast false life on yourself at will as a 1st-level psi, without expending a psi slot or material components.

Gaze of Two Minds

You can use your action to touch a willing humanoid and perceive through its senses until the end of your next turn. As long as the creature is on the same plane of existence as you, you can use your action on subsequent turns to maintain this connection, extending the duration until the end of your next turn. While perceiving through the other creature's senses, you benefit from any special senses possessed by that creature, and you are blinded and deafened to your own surroundings.

Lifedrinker

Prerequisite: 12th level, Pact of the Blade feature

When you hit a creature with your pact weapon, the creature takes extra necrotic damage equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1).

Mask of Many Faces

You can cast disguise self at will, without expending a psi slot.

Master of Myriad Forms

Prerequisite: 15th level

You can cast alter self at will, without expending a psi slot.

Minions of Chaos

Prerequisite: 9th level

You can cast conjure elemental once using a Symbiote psi slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.

Mire the Mind

Prerequisite: 5th level

You can cast slow once using a Symbiote psi slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.

Misty Visions

You can cast silent image at will, without expending a psi slot or material components.

One with Shadows

Prerequisite: 5th level

When you are in an area of dim light or darkness, you can use your action to become invisible until you move or take an action or a reaction.

Otherworldly Leap

Prerequisite: 9th level

You can cast jump on yourself at will, without expending a psi slot or material components.

Repelling Blast

Prerequisite: eldritch blast cantrip When you hit a creature with eldritch blast, you can push the creature up to 10 feet away from you in a straight line.

Sculptor of Flesh

Prerequisite: 7th level

You can cast polymorph once using a Symbiote psi slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.

Sign of Ill Omen

Prerequisite: 5th level

You can cast bestow curse once using a Symbiote psi slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.

Thief of Five Fates

You can cast bane once using a Symbiote psi slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.

Thirsting Blade

Prerequisite: 5th level, Pact of the Blade feature

You can attack with your pact weapon twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn. Visions of Distant Realms Prerequisite: 15th level You can cast arcane eye at will, without expending a psi slot.

Voice of the Chain Master

Prerequisite: Pact of the Chain feature You can communicate telepathically with your familiar and perceive through your familiar's senses as long as you are on the same plane of existence. Additionally, while perceiving through your familiar's senses, you can also speak through your familiar in your own voice, even if your familiar is normally incapable of speech.

Whispers of the Grave

Prerequisite: 9th level You can cast speak with dead at will, without expending a psi slot.

Witch Sight

Prerequisite: 15th level You can see the true form of any shapechanger or creature concealed by illusion or transmutation psionics while the creature is within 30 feet of you and within line of sight.

Otherworldly Patrons

The beings that serve as patrons for Symbiotes are mighty inhabitants of other planes of existence—not gods, but almost godlike in their power. Various patrons give their Symbiotes access to different powers and invocations, and expect significant favors in return.
Some patrons collect Symbiotes, doling out mystic knowledge relatively freely or boasting of their ability to bind mortals to their will. Other patrons bestow their power only grudgingly, and might make a pact with only one Symbiote. Symbiotes who serve the same patron might view each other as allies, siblings, or rivals.

The Fiend

You have made a pact with a fiend from the lower planes of existence, a being whose aims are evil, even if you strive against those aims. Such beings desire the corruption or destruction of all things, ultimately including you. Fiends powerful enough to forge a pact include demon lords such as Demogorgon, Orcus, Fraz'Urb-luu, and Baphomet; archdevils such as Asmodeus, Dispater, Mephistopheles, and Belial; pit fiends and balors that are especially mighty; and ultroloths and other lords of the yugoloths.

Expanded psi List

The Fiend lets you choose from an expanded list of psi powers when you learn a Symbiote psi. The following psi powers are added to the Symbiote psi list for you.

psi Level psi powers
1st burning hands, command
2nd blindness/deafness, scorching ray
3rd fireball, stinking cloud
4th fire shield, wall of fire
5th flame strike, hallow

Dark One's Blessing

Starting at 1st level, when you reduce a hostile creature to 0 hit points, you gain temporary hit points equal to your Charisma modifier + your Symbiote level (minimum of 1).

Dark One's Own Luck

Starting at 6th level, you can call on your patron to alter fate in your favor. When you make an ability check or a saving throw, you can use this feature to add a d10 to your roll. You can do so after seeing the initial roll but before any of the roll's effects occur.
Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

Fiendish Resilience

Starting at 10th level, you can choose one damage type when you finish a short or long rest. You gain resistance to that damage type until you choose a different one with this feature. Damage from psionic weapons or silver weapons ignores this resistance.

Hurl Through Hell

Starting at 14th level, when you hit a creature with an attack, you can use this feature to instantly transport the target through the lower planes. The creature disappears and hurtles through a nightmare landscape.
At the end of your next turn, the target returns to the space it previously occupied, or the nearest unoccupied space. If the target is not a fiend, it takes 10d10 psychic damage as it reels from its horrific experience.
Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.

Your Pact Boon

Each Pact Boon option produces a special creature or an object that reflects your patron's nature.
Pact of the Chain. Your familiar is more cunning than a typical familiar. Its default form can be a reflection of your patron, with sprites and pseudodragons tied to the Archfey and imps and quasits tied to the Fiend. Because the Great Old One's nature is inscrutable, any familiar form is suitable for it.
Pact of the Blade. If your patron is the Archfey, your weapon might be a slender blade wrapped in leafy vines. If you serve the Fiend, your weapon could be an axe made of black metal and adorned with decorative flames. If your patron is the Great Old One, your weapon might be an ancient-looking spear, with a gemstone embedded in its head, carved to look like a terrible unblinking eye.
Pact of the Tome. Your Book of Shadows might be a fine, gilt-edged tome with psi powers of enchantment and illusion, gifted to you by the lordly Archfey. It could be a weighty tome bound in demon hide studded with iron, holding psi powers of conjuration and a wealth of forbidden lore about the sinister regions of the cosmos, a gift of the Fiend. Or it could be the tattered diary of a lunatic driven mad by contact with the Great Old One, holding scraps of psi powers that only your own burgeoning insanity allows you to understand and cast.
